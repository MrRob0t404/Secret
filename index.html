Rules & Regulations

  1. Every participant must bring a wrapped gift (recommended price ~$10)
  2. Participant order will be randomized
  3. First participant selects a gift and opens it
  4. Following participants can choose to either steal a previous gift or pick a new gift. Participants who gets their gift stolen can either pick a new gift or steal from someone else
  5. A gift can only be stolen once per turn
  6. After all participants have gone, the first player gets a chance to steal from any other person. The game ends when someone declines to steal a gift
